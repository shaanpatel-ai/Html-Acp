About codingal

Codingal is a top-rated online after-school platform providing live 1-on-1, AI-powered coding classes for kids aged 5-17 (grades 1-12). It aims to foster a love for coding through a STEM-accredited, project-based curriculum covering Python, Scratch, App Development, and AI, preparing over 1 million students globally for future technology careers.
