The New 7 Wonders of the World, announced in 2007 following a global poll, include the Great Wall of China, Petra, the Colosseum, Chichén Itzá, Machu Picchu, the Taj Mahal, and Christ the Redeemer. These iconic landmarks were chosen to represent global cultural heritage, highlighting enduring architectural feats.
